In this project I learned how to display images in different projections

A map of the world was altered based on different projections in QGIS using the Indicatrix mapper to model the distortions. The projections themselves were altered by changing the Coordinate Reference System(CRS).


WGS84 Projection

preserves direction

Pseudo Mercador Projection

preserves shape, direction

World Cyllindrical Equal Area Projection

preserve area, direction

North Pole Azimuthal Equidistant Projection

preserve direction and distance

World From Space Projection

mostly preserves all, cannot be fully viewed in 2D

Aitoff Projection

preserves area

Sphere Winkel I

compromises on all

World Equidistant Conic Projection

preserve distance

Wagner IV Projection

preserve area
